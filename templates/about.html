About To-Do List

Introduction

Welcome to our To-Do List application! Our app is designed to help you stay organized and manage your tasks effectively.

How to Use

  1. Create a new task by entering information in input boxes.
  2. Enter a title and description for the task.
  3. Click on Submit button.
  4. Update tasks by clicking on the "Update" button.
  5. Delete tasks by clicking on the "Delete" button.
